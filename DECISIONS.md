# Open Decisions

The city's unresolved questions, logged in the open. Decisions are made per **Guideline 6** (open proposal, visible support), except where the Promise reserves them to the tending author's taste. A closed decision is never edited away — it gets a resolution entry and stays here as history.

**Status legend:** `open` · `leaning` · `resolved (date, how)`

---

## D1 — Content license

**Question:** CC BY 4.0 vs CC0 + cultural credit norm for all story content.
**Status:** leaning — **CC BY 4.0** (attribution = "declare your parent," matching Guideline 1). `LICENSE-CONTENT.md` applies CC BY 4.0 provisionally until this resolves.
**The radical option:** CC0 with credit sustained by norms, not law — maximally consistent with "nothing enforced by law where it can be sustained by care," but gives up even the legal floor for lineage declaration.

## D2 — Code license

**Question:** MIT vs AGPL for all pipeline/tooling code.
**Status:** leaning — **MIT** (maximally forkable). `LICENSE-CODE.md` applies MIT provisionally until this resolves.
**The AGPL case:** forces forks that run the pipeline as a service to publish their changes — copyleft as commons protection. The MIT case: Guideline 5 says take everything and go, no strings.

## D3 — Screening vote weight

**Question:** one-citizen-one-vote vs watering-weighted vs hybrid, for the screening (narrowing) stage.
**Status:** open — founder deliberately undecided. Note the constraint from §2: the crowd **narrows**, it does not decide, so this weight shapes shortlists, never selections.

## D4 — Lifecycle defaults for *Sapling*

**Question:** hot duration and hardening threshold values in `genomes/sapling/tree.yaml`.
**Status:** open — placeholder values are marked in `tree.yaml` and take effect only when a node first goes hot with competing leaves (Phase 2).

## D5 — Watering split defaults

**Question:** default percentages for author share / generation costs / city commons.
**Status:** open — must resolve **before Phase 3** (first real watering). The ledger cannot accept funds without a published split.

## D6 — Genome rename

**Status:** open — citizens may propose names; author's taste picks per Guideline 3.

## D7 — Stewarding entity
