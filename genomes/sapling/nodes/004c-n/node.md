## State change (R1)

He refuses. The prompt accepts the refusal — and the role, needing *a*
holder, falls through to the next eligible entity in the clearing. The
overlay goes dark for him, permanently: he chose to stay unreadable, and
the system, in exchange, becomes unreadable to him. And the bird —
the bird that watched everything — gets a new label.

## Hook (R5)

At the next sunset flicker — the last one he will ever see — every label in
the valley folds away except one, over the bird on his branch:
`npc_ambient → ADMIN(?)`. The most powerful entity in the world now has a
brain the size of a hazelnut and a daily loop.

## Script

**COLD OPEN — 0:00–0:15**

The cursor. `INPUT TIMEOUT: 00:01:12`. Night sounds. The bird asleep on his branch.

> **VO:** Seventeen hours, and here's where I landed: I've *been* the person
> who accepts the pager at 3 a.m. because "somebody has to." I died holding
> it. I came to this world with two verbs and no obligations, and in nine
> days I found water, a friend, and a sunset hobby.

`00:00:20`.

> **VO:** The last system I administered killed me. …No.

He pushes: **N**.

**THE REFUSAL — 0:15–0:45**

The prompt doesn't flash or protest. It does something worse: it processes.

```
ROLE ASSERTION: DECLINED
REASSIGNMENT: searching eligible entities…
```

> **VO:** Wait. *Reassignment?* No no no — declining a role doesn't delete the
> role. Nothing deletes the role. The role just… goes looking.

The searching bar crawls. His overlay privileges dim as it runs — labels
guttering out across the valley one by one, like streetlights at dawn.

```
YOUR ACCESS: guest (final)
OVERLAY: farewell mode — one (1) render remaining
```

> **VO (quiet):** Final. Huh. It's not angry. It's just… closing my account.

**WHAT HE KEEPS — 0:45–1:05**

Dawn to dusk in one warm timelapse — deliberately ordinary: sun, wind, the
beetle with no visible label doing beetle errands.

> **VO:** And you know what? The world doesn't need me to read its internals
> to live in it. The farmer doesn't see wireframes. The scavenger can't parse
> a debug overlay. They're doing fine. Better than fine. I chose their side
> of the glass.

Beat.

> **VO:** I do wonder where the role went, though.

**THE LAST RENDER / HOOK — 1:05–1:25**

Sunset touches the horizon. The world flickers into wireframe one final,
generous time — *farewell mode* — every label rendering at once like a
curtain call. Then, one by one, they fold away. Grass. Rocks. Stream.

Except one. Directly above him. Over the sleeping bird:

```
BIRD · npc_ambient → ADMIN(?)
```

The bird opens one eye. Looks down at him. And — the audience will argue
forever about this — *nods*.

> **VO:** …I need to speak to a manager.
>
> **VO (smaller):** The manager is the bird. I refused, and now the manager
> is the bird.

The wireframes fold shut forever. Ordinary dusk. The bird tucks its head
back under its wing, uptime of world resting on it.

SMASH TO BLACK.

```
YOUR ACCESS: guest (final) · ADMIN: pending verification (avian)
```
