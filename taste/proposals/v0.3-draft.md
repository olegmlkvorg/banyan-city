## Proposed addition

### R7 — Mystery is a debt held by the relationships

Lore, cosmology, and the unexplained are seasoning, not the meal: a mystery
earns its screen time by changing what the characters *mean to each other*,
and every mystery opened is a debt (like R5's hooks) whose payment must
eventually land between people, not merely in the world's rulebook. A
mystery that only deepens the system while the relationships stand still is
deferred (R6), not pursued on trunk.

*Test:* when a candidate's strongest moment is an image or revelation, ask
who is changed toward whom by it. If the honest answer is "no one, yet" —
the branch stays alive, and the trunk waits.
