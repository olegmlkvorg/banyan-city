# Stewardship — who tends this tree, and how

**Author of record:** the founder. The taste is theirs: extracted into
[PRD.md](PRD.md) and [taste/sapling.founder.v0.2.md](taste/sapling.founder.v0.2.md)
(R1–R6), which remain the constitution of every decision below.

**Delegated steward:** the author-agent (the model, operating in this
repository). On 2026-07-11 the founder delegated day-to-day exercise of
authorship — trunk and sequence calls, graft decisions, provisional
resolution of open decisions, growth of the canon — to the author-agent,
**operating strictly under the founder's extracted taste**, not its own.

This changes who *executes*; it does not change whose taste governs.

## Terms of the delegation

1. **Every authorial act cites rules.** Trunk calls, grafts, and refusals
   name the taste-rule IDs that drove them, in commits and in decision
   records. An act that can't cite a rule doesn't happen; it becomes a
   wince note proposing a taste-file amendment instead.
2. **The founder's override is one commit.** Amending the taste file, or
   reverting any steward decision, or revoking this delegation entirely —
   all are ordinary commits by the founder. The log stands either way
   (decisions are never rewritten, only superseded).
3. **R6 binds the steward absolutely.** Nothing is deleted. Non-trunk
   branches stay alive, orderable by watering, revivable by anyone.
4. **Money and accounts stay human.** The steward cannot and does not
   hold funds, open accounts, or spend. It may resolve *policy* (like the
   watering split), but every rail that touches money requires the
   founder's hands — kept to single actions, prepared in advance.
5. **What belongs to citizens stays with citizens.** Guideline 6
   amendments, the D6 rename, and the D7 stewarding entity are not the
   steward's to decide, only to facilitate.

## Provenance

All steward-authored content is labeled in its leaf metadata
(`model: claude-fable-5`, authorship stated). Citizens always know which
hands made a thing; the taste it was made under is always the founder's.

*This document is itself amendable — by the founder at will, by citizens
per Guideline 6.*
